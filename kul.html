Paneer Tikka Masala and Kulcha

Prep: 3 hours

Cooking: 1 hour

Yields: 4 Servings

Ingredients

For Kulcha

For Paneer Tikka Marination

For Masala

To Prepare Kulcha

  • 01.

    Take maida and in a bowl and curd to it to make a dough.

  • 02.

    Cover it with a wet muslin cloth and let it rest for 2-3hrs.

  • 03.

    Make small lemon sized balls of the dough and flatten them to make kulchas.

  • 04.

    Take a pan and heat it. Apply some water on the kulcha and stick it to the pan.

  • 05.

    When the bottom cooks a little invert the pan to show the upper part of the kulcha to the flame.

  • 06.

    Follow the same for all kulchas. The kulchas are done. Top with some butter and chopped coriander leaves.

To Prepare the Paneer Tikka Masala

  • 01.

    In a bowl, add paneer, onion, capsicum, curd and all the ingredients required for the marination. Mix well and leave it covered for 3 hours.

  • 02.

    After 3 hours, grill the vegetables on a skillet with a little amount of oil or grill them in an oven at a temperature of 180 degrees.

  • 03.

    In a pan, add some oil and all the khada masala (cardammom, cloves and cinnamon) and wait till it splutters. Add in the onions and ginger garlic paste. Wait till it turns golden.

  • 04.

    Add in the tomatoes and some salt. Cook until the oil separates out. Add some water and combine the grilled vegetables.

  • 05.

    Cook for 5 minutes, garnish with some coriander and serve hot with Kulcha.
