Quick and Easy Cupcakes

Prep: 15 mins

Baking: 120 mins

Oven: Preheat 160C: 10 mins

Yields: 4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 01.

    First separate the cream from the biscuits and add the biscuits into a blender.

  • 02.

    Add baking soda and baking powder into this mixture and transfer to a bowl. Add in the milk to form a thick liquidy batter.

  • 03.

    Line the cupcake tray with cupcake liners and add the batter into it. Bake at 180 degrees for 15-20 minutes and then remove them from the oven.

  • 04.

    Take the melted chocolate and pour it into a mould of your choice. Let it cool and then place it on your cupcakes. Ready to serve!
