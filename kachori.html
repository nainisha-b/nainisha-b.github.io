Khasta Kachori

Prep: 15 mins

Cooling: 30 mins

Yields: 6-10 servings

Ingredients

For Stuffing

For Dough

    To prepare the Stuffing

  • 01.

    In a kadai dry roast moong dal and make a coarse powder.

  • 02.

    Turn off the flame, and transfer to the pan/bowl.

  • 03.

    Add the spices and mix the mixture uniformly.

  • 04.

    Now slowly add the water and gradualy mix the stuffing.

  • 05.

    Add water until you get a mud like texture. Your stuffing is ready to go.

    • To prepare the dough

  • 01.

    In a large mixing bowl add maida, rava, oil, soda(optional) and salt.

  • 02.

    Crumble the flour mix with oil.

  • 03.

    Knead the crumble to form a dough by adding water slowly.

  • 04.

    Cover the dough with a wet cloth and it rest for about 15 to 20 mins. The dough is done.

    • To make Kachoris

  • 01.

    With the help of both of your thumbs start pressing the edges and create a dent in centre

  • 02.

    Thin the edges and add some of the stuffing.

  • 03.

    Now put some stuffing in the dough and secure the edges.

  • 04.

    Form a bundle, close the kachori and flatten it.

  • 05.

    Thin the sides, do the same with all the kachoris.

  • 06.

    When the oil is medium hot add in one or two kackoris at a time for deep frying.

  • 07.

    Don't touch it for a minute or till they float. Then press with the spoon to puff up. Turn around and fry the kachori till it is golden brown all over.

  • 08.

    Serve the kachori hot along with green chutney.
