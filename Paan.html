Paan Ice-Cream

Prep: 10 mins

Cooling: 120 mins

Yields: 8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 01.

    In a blender add chopped paan leaves, gulkand and the saunf. Blend the mixture to form a thick green paste.

  • 02.

    Take the vanilla ice-cream in a large mixing bowl, let the ice-cream melt and then add the paan mixture to it. Mix both well to form a creamy green mixture.

  • 03.

    Add this ice-cream to a glass bowl and freeze it in the freezer for atleast two hours.

  • 04.

    Once the ice-cream sets, scoop out some ice-cream, put it in a bowl. Add a cherry on top and serve cold. Your delicious paan ice-cream is ready.
