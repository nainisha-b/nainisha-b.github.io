American Chopsuey

Prep: 20 mins

Cooking: 45 mins

Yields: 4 Servings

Ingredients

For Sauce

For Noodles

To Prepare Noodles

  • 01.

    Heat oil in a pan.

  • 02.

    Take the boiled noodles, coat them in some cornflour and start frying in batches on low flame.

  • 03.

    This might take a lot of time so be patient and start preparing the sauce. You can remove the noodle once they are golden brown.

To Prepare Sauce

  • 01.

    In a pan, add some oil. Once the oil heats up, add some chopped garlic. After the garlic turns light yellow add the sliced onions.

  • 02.

    Fry until golden and add the julliened vegetables(capsicum, carrot, cabbage). Cover the pan and let it simmer for 2-3 minutes.

  • 03.

    Now add some salt, chilli powder, the soya sauce and tomato ketchup. Mix all the ingredients well.

  • 04.

    In a separate boil take cornflour and water. Mix them together to form a slurry paste.

  • 05.

    Add this mixture into the wok and mix the ingredients well. Add some more water to form a thick soupy sauce.

  • 06.

    Add in sugar and some vinegar/lemon juice.

  • 07.

    To finish add some chopped spring onions and mix the sauce.

  • 08.

    To serve, first add the fried noodles ina bowl. Add the sauce on top such that it covers the noodles. American Chopsuey is ready to serve!
