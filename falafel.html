Khasta Kachori

Prep: 12 hours

Cooling: 30 mins

Yields: 10 to 12 servings

Ingredients

  • 01.

    In a bowl add the soaked chikpeas, coriander, onions, garlic and the spices{chilli powder, salt, jeera powder, Coriander powder}

  • 02.

    Now add Maida in this.

  • 03.

    Transfer to a food processor or mixer grinder.

  • 04.

    Blend coarsely.Don't add water

  • 05.

    Now in this add baking soda and mix well.

  • 06.

    Now using two spoons make lunches{Triangular prism like}.

  • 07.

    Heat the oil and transfer each of the falafels in it for deep frying.

  • 08.

    Stir occasionally. Keep the flame on medium.

  • 09.

    Remove the falafels onto a kitchen paper when they are golden brown.

  • 10.

    Your falafel are ready to eat. Serve hot with Green chutney or hummus.
