Aloo Ragada

Prep: 12 hrs (incudes soaking time)

Cooling: 90 mins

Yields: 6 servngs

Ingredients

For Tikki

For Chana masala

For chat

    For Tikki

  • 01.

    In a mixing bowl, mash the boiled potatoes.

  • 02.

    In the potatoes add the salt, chilli powder, chat masala, coriander, green chillies and combine well.

  • 03.

    Make circular tikkis of any size that you desire.

  • 04.

    Heat up a pan and pour some oil{suffient for shallow frying}.

  • 05.

    Slowly fry the tikkis in the oil.

  • 06.

    Fry on both sides and transfer to a plate lined with kitchen paper.

  • 07.

    Your tikki is ready.

    • For Chana masala

  • 01.

    Boil the soaked white peas along with cinnamon stick, bay leaf, cloves and cardamom.

  • 02.

    In a pan add oil

  • 03.

    Once the oil is heated add ginger paste, red chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder and turmeric(Add a little oil to masalas so that they won't burn)

  • 04.

    Add the boiled batanas(along with water) to this masala and let it cook until the oil separates

  • 05.

    Add garam masala. Garnish with chopped coriander. Your chana masala is ready

    • To assemble the chat

  • 01.

    In a plate place one or two tikkis at the bottom.

  • 02.

    Top it with some of the chana masala, chopped onions, and chopped tomatoes.

  • 03.

    Add some curd, coriander chutney, tamarind chutney and top it with sev and papdi. Your aloo ragada is ready!
