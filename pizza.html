Tandoori Pizza

Prep: 3 hours {includes proofing time}

Cooking: 15 mins

Yields: 6-10 servings

Ingredients

For Dough

For Sauce

Toppings

    To prepare the Dough

  • 01.

    Activate the yeast by adding the yeast to the warm water and add sugar.

  • 02.

    In a bowl add the maida, salt and activated yeast to form a soft and sticky dough.

  • 03.

    Apply some oil over it and cover it with a wet muslin cloth or plastic food wrap.

  • 04.

    Let it rest for three to four hours or until it doubles in size.

  • 05.

    Since it will take time to rest lets prepare the sauce.

    • To prepare the sauce

  • 01.

    In a bowl add tomato puree,kechup,mayonese,and chilli sauce.
    *You can adjust the sweetness or spiceness according to your taste

  • 02.

    To this add finely chopped basil and oregano.

  • 03.

    Heat it in a pan and adjust the thickness by adding water.(This step is OPTIONAL)

  • 04.

    Your sauce is ready.

    • To make the pizza

  • 01.

    punch the dough to release the air. Divide the dough into four or six parts.

  • 02.

    Take one part and make the crust with desired thickness.(I'd prefer Thin)

  • 03.

    Poke some holes with the help of a fork.

  • 04.

    Place the base in a preheated oven for 2-3minutes.

  • 05.

    Remove the base and add the sauce over it.

  • 06.

    Now add all the toppings and cheese.

  • 07.

    Pop it in the oven for 10 to 15 minutes or until its base is cooked.

  • 08.

    Cut the pizza in sectors. Enjoy your Indian Italian meal!

  • Mum's TIP

    Coat the onion and bell peppers in tandoori mayonese and then add to pizza. This will give it a Better tandoori taste
