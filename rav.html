Spinach and Ricotta Ravioli

Prep: 20 mins

Cooking: 45 mins

Yields: 4 Servings

Ingredients

For Ravioli

To Make Dough for the Ravioli

  • 01.

    In a bowl, mix maida, sooji, salt with water to form a tight dough

  • 02.

    Let the dough rest for some time. Cover it with a wet cloth

To Make Dough for the Ravioli

  • 01.

    In a bowl, mix maida, sooji, salt with water to form a tight dough

  • 02.

    Let the dough rest for some time. Cover it with a wet cloth

To Make The Stuffing

  • 01.

    Chop the blanched spinach and add it in a bowl. Add some crumbled Paneer or Riccota and mix them together.

  • 02.

    Add some salt, crushed pepper, the dry herbs and some chilli flakes.

  • 03.

    Roll out the dough into a thin sheet and using a cookie cutter cut the dough into circles. Put a spoon of the stuffing and cover in with another circle. Using a fork seal the edges.

  • 04.

    Add these prepared ravioli in a pan of boiling hot water(add in some salt and oil). Cook the ravioli until they become translucent i.e 3-4 minutes. Remove them and put them on a plate.

To Prepare Sauce

  • 01.

    In a pan, add some oil. Once the oil heats up, add some chopped garlic. After the garlic turns light yellow add chopped oninons.

  • 02.

    Add in the chopped capsicum. After some time add in the sauces and tomato puree. Add some salt, dry herbs and chilli flakes. If theres any stuffing left you can add it in as well.

  • 03.

    In a bowl mix some cornflour and water. Add it in to the sauce to make it thick. Add in the olives as well.

  • 04.

    Add in the cooked ravioli add gently mix. Try not to break the ravioli as they are extremely fragile.

  • 05.

    The ravioli is ready to serve. You can garnish it with a good amount of parmesan cheese.
