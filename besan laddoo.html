Besan Ke Laddoo

Prep: 10 mins

Cooking: 15-20 mins

Yields: 20 to 25 laddoos

Ingredients

  • 01.

    Heat a flat bottom pan and add ghee.

  • 02.

    Add besan and Roast until light brown.

  • 03.

    Transfer it in an other plate and let it cool.

  • 04.

    After sometime add the sugar and mix well.

    • NOTE: DON'T ADD THE SUGAR TO MIXTURE WHEN IT IS HOT. The sugar will caramelize and we will not get the crumbling texture.

  • 05.

    Now quickly make laddoos of desired size.

  • 06.

    Place a pistatio on each of them.

  • 07.

    Your besan ke laddoo are ready to eat!
