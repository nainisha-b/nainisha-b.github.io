Oatmeal Cookies

Prep: 10 mins

Oven: 180 C (20 mins)

Yields: Approx-20 cookies

Ingredients

  • 01.

    In a mixing bowl, combine brown sugar, white sugar, honey and butter (at room temperature).

  • 02.

    In another bowl, mix the dry ingrediants- oats,maida,baking powder,baking soda and cinnamon powder.

  • 03.

    Add the dry ingrediants to the wet ingrediants.

  • 04.

    Now fold in the raisins to form a sticky dough.

  • 05.

    Keep the dough in the refrigerator for about half an hour.

  • 06.

    Take the dough out, make small balls and flatten it to get the shape of the cookie.

  • 07.

    Place them on a baking tray and put it in a preheated oven for 15 to 20 min or until golden brown.

  • 08.

    Cool and enjoy. Store in an air-tight container.These cookies will for 10 to 15 days unless you complete them in one day!
